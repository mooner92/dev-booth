## Learnings
- WS payload size: each comment row ~200 bytes; 100-entry cap = ~20 KB/push.
- The flex-min-height gotcha: flex/grid children default to `min-height: auto`
  which means they won't shrink below intrinsic min-content height. Tailwind's
  `min-h-0` is the fix. Bug was on 3 ancestors at once — all three needed it.
- task_comments table on v0.13.0: id INTEGER PK, task_id TEXT, author TEXT,
  body TEXT, created_at INTEGER (seconds-epoch). One LEFT JOIN with `tasks`
  yields task_title + task_assignee for "kind=comment" LogEntry projection.
